Is the music of our generation

When it comes to music, you’ll be very hard-pressed to find anyone alive who doesn’t enjoy music in some way or form. There’s always a genre out there that speaks to us, and the world would be a much duller place if music wasn’t there to give it some color. The history of music is rich and proud, with so many stand-out genres that it’s almost impossible to list them all. There are some genres however that have a habit of bringing people together more so than others - with one of the best examples being hip-hop.
